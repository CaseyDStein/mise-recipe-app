Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

A focused set of tools that makes building and browsing your recipe collection effortless.

Import from any URL

Paste a link from any cooking website — AllRecipes, Serious Eats, Bon Appétit, and thousands more. The app handles the rest.

Smart Search

Find any saved recipe by title, ingredient, cuisine, or description. Partial matches work too — just type what you remember.

Tags & Organization

Tag your recipes any way you like — by meal type, diet, occasion, or season. Cuisine tags are added automatically on import.

Auto-extracted Nutrition

Calories, protein, carbs, fat, fiber, and sodium are pulled from the recipe source whenever available.

Share to Import

Browsing recipes in Safari or Chrome? Use the share button to send it straight to the app — no copy-pasting required.

Light & Dark Mode

The app automatically matches your device's appearance setting, so it always looks exactly how you'd expect.

How It Works

From browser to your
collection in seconds

1

Find a recipe you want to save

Browse any cooking website on your phone or desktop — AllRecipes, Food Network, Serious Eats, New York Times Cooking, or any food blog with a recipe on the page.

2

Paste the URL or share directly

Copy and paste the recipe URL into the app, or tap the share button in your browser and choose The Recipe Organizer. The app fetches and parses the page automatically.

3

Your recipe is saved and ready

Ingredients, step-by-step instructions, cook times, servings, and nutritional info are all extracted and stored. Search it, tag it, and cook from it — all in one place.
